Project foundation

Project overview

Recovering from an injury can be a long and frustrating process. This UX/UI project explores how digital tools can support people during rehabilitation by helping them stay consistent with physiotherapy exercises, track their progress, and stay motivated.

Overview

Many people recovering from injuries struggle to stay consistent with their rehabilitation exercises. Recovery often takes several months, and patients may lose motivation or forget their routines during the process.

Focus areas

  • Clear exercise guidance
  • Progress tracking and recovery insights
  • Daily wellbeing reflections
  • Stress-reducing mindfulness support

Goal

The goal was to create a calm and supportive digital experience that addresses both the physical and emotional aspects of recovery.

Target user

User Persona

To guide the design process, the primary persona was created based on the target users.

Chris – 37, Designer

Chris is a 37-year-old designer and former competitive swimmer recovering from shoulder surgery. He wants to regain strength and mobility but struggles to stay consistent with his rehabilitation exercises.

Needs

  • To visually track recovery progress
  • To receive reminders for daily exercises
  • To use a simple and efficient app that fits his routine
  • To stay motivated during recovery
  • To easily share progress with his physiotherapist

Goals

  • Regain full shoulder mobility
  • Maintain consistency with rehabilitation exercises
  • Track measurable recovery progress
  • Reduce stress during the rehabilitation process

Frustrations

  • Slow progress after surgery
  • Forgetting to complete exercises
  • Feeling discouraged during recovery
Note: This project was based on a predefined persona provided in the assignment.
Problem and direction

App Goals (based on user needs)

Key insights from the persona analysis revealed that the app needed to:

  • Provide clear and simple guidance for daily exercises
  • Offer visual feedback to motivate progress
  • Support emotional wellbeing during recovery
  • Create a calm and intuitive user experience

Final direction

FINAL DESIGN

The final interface focuses on simplicity, clear navigation, and motivational feedback to support users throughout their rehabilitation journey.

Design priorities:

  • Simple and intuitive navigation
  • Clear visual hierarchy to support quick decision-making
  • Motivational progress feedback to encourage consistency
  • Easy access to exercises and recovery insights

Core features:

  • Recovery progress dashboard with visual feedback
  • Exercise library with guided physiotherapy sessions
  • Wellbeing and reflection tracking
  • Guided breathing and relaxation exercises

Design approach:

  • Calm and supportive color palette
  • Clear and accessible typography
  • Minimal interface to reduce cognitive load and improve focus
Reflection

Outcome

The final result is a high-fidelity mobile prototype that combines recovery tracking, exercise guidance, and wellbeing support in one experience. The design demonstrates how a supportive and simple interface can help users stay consistent with rehabilitation exercises while tracking their recovery progress.

Key Learnings

  • Consistency matters: Daily engagement supports successful recovery.
  • Motivation drives progress: Visual feedback encourages continued use.
  • Simplicity improves usability: Clear navigation reduces cognitive load.
  • Recovery is holistic: Physical rehabilitation benefits from mental wellbeing support.
