About me

Figma User Research Prototyping Accessibility Wireframing Usability Testing

As a Stockholm-based UX Designer, I recently completed my studies at Noroff School of Technology and Digital Media.
During my time there, I’ve built a strong foundation in designing digital environments that are both inclusive and user-focused.

I believe that the best solutions come from a mix of deep user research and creative problem-solving. My goal is always to turn complicated data into seamless, everyday interactions.

With the experience of bringing digital products from the classroom into the professional world, I am now eager to tackle new challenges.
